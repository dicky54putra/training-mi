Our 3M principle

1Method

Our many years of research have led to a method in which there is a statistical dvantage (edge) that can be exploited continuously.

2Mindset

The weakest link is usually the investor or trader himself. Investing is making decisions. Trading is making decisions even more frequently. Since one only knows afterwards whether a decision is right, trading requires dealing with uncertainty in the right way. The mindset is essential for achieving success. Many investors have difficulty accepting losses and therefore often wait too long.

3Money Management

Since no one can predict the future, even the best investor or trader will make mistakes. An erroneous estimate leads to a loss. This does not have to be a problem as long as this loss is limited and in the right proportion to the profit transactions. Our approach has a good risk/return ratio that is often much better than that of other investments. Good risk management ensures the limited drawdown in our results.
